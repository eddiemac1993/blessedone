Delivery Application

In addition to the about page, our website offers a delivery application where customers can place their orders for delivery. Here's how it works:

  1. Go to the menu and browse the items available for ordering.
  2. Select the items you want by clicking the small box next to each item.
  3. Fill out the form with your name, reference ID (if applicable), delivery location, and delivery fee.
  4. Submit your order by clicking the "Submit Order" button.
  5. A confirmation drop-down menu will appear to verify your order.
  6. If everything is correct, you can download the invoice if you wish, but you'll still need to complete the transaction by sending the order through the WhatsApp button.
  7. Our orders are cash on delivery, so you'll pay when the delivery is made.

If you have any questions or concerns, our support team is always available to help. Thank you for using our delivery application!
