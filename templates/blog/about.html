About Us

BlessedTouch

BlessedTouch Multipurpose Cooperative Society is a cooperative dedicated to providing support and assistance to both the youth and the elderly. Our cooperative aims to educate, empower, and uplift individuals in our community by offering a range of services that cater to their specific needs. We believe in fostering a sense of collaboration, inclusivity, and social responsibility in all our endeavors. Through our website, we provide free ad posting services and facilitate online ordering and delivery of various products. By partnering with like-minded organizations, we strive to create a strong network for seamless operations and impactful work.

Our Story

BlessedTouch Multipurpose Cooperative Society was founded with the belief that everyone deserves opportunities for personal and professional growth, regardless of age. Recognizing the potential of both the youth and the elderly, we sought to create a platform that would connect them, fostering mutual learning and support. Our cooperative was formed by a group of passionate individuals who have firsthand experience in community development and a deep commitment to making a positive difference.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a society where the youth and the elderly can thrive together, building a harmonious and interconnected community. We envision a world where education, entrepreneurship, and support systems are readily available, enabling individuals to achieve their full potential. By leveraging technology and collaboration, we aim to bridge the gap between generations and empower individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Our Mission
  • Offering educational programs and workshops that enhance skills and knowledge for personal and professional growth.
  • Assisting small businesses by providing mentorship, access to resources, and networking opportunities.
  • Operating a user-friendly website, BlessedTouch, where individuals can post ads for free, promoting their products and services.
  • Facilitating convenient online ordering and delivery services to meet the diverse needs of our community.
  • Establishing strategic partnerships with organizations, businesses, and individuals who share our values and objectives.
Our Team

Our cooperative is led by a dedicated team of individuals who possess expertise in community development, business management, and technology. Each team member brings unique skills and experiences to the table, ensuring effective planning, execution, and growth. We also engage volunteers and professionals who contribute their time and expertise to our initiatives, creating a diverse and passionate network.

Our Values
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of collective efforts and collaboration to achieve meaningful impact.
  • Inclusivity: We strive to create an inclusive environment that values and respects the diversity of our community.
  • Empowerment: We empower individuals by providing them with the tools, resources, and opportunities necessary for personal and professional growth.
  • Integrity: We conduct our operations with integrity, transparency, and ethical standards.
  • Social Responsibility: We are committed to making a positive social impact and giving back to our community.
