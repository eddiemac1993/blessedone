About Us

We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to providing high-quality, locally-sourced sausages and excellent customer service. Our goal is to create a memorable shopping experience for our customers and to constantly improve and evolve with the times.

Our History

Founded in 2023, we started as a small online store selling handmade sausages. As our business grew, we expanded our offerings to include a wide range of products for our customers. We have always put our customers first and strive to provide them with the best possible experience.

Our Team

Our team is made up of a diverse group of individuals with a passion for providing excellent customer service and creating high-quality products. From our sausage makers and packaging team to our customer service representatives, each member of our team plays an important role in the success of our cooperative.

Our Values

  • Customer satisfaction
  • Quality products
  • Continuous improvement
  • Community engagement
